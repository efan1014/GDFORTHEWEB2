FAVORITES

    1. RACIAL TRIANGULATION......................................$10

The positioning of Asian Americans by white supremacy as being superior enough to replace Black Americans but too foregin to achieve any "real American" status

    2. MODEL MINORITY MYTH.......................................$10

The myth that Asian Americans are more socio-economically successful than other minorities in America.

    3. ASIAN AMERICAN QUEERNESS..................................$10

My observation that in queerness we are able to find alternative identities and methods of expression that neither seek to imitate/please whiteness through the model minority myth nor appropriate blackness.

    4. "ALIENS INELIGIBLE FOR CITIZENSHIP" (ASSIMILATION)........$10

The othering and stereotyping of Asian bodies in America as attempts to emphasize our foreignness and supposedly unassimilable nature.

    5. FETISHIZATION & OBJECTIFICATION...........................$10

The fetishizing and feminizing of our bodies for the purpose of implying easy dominance over us and a consumable nature.
