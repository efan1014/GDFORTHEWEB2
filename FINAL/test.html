( : SAD ASIAN GIRLS )

STARTERS

1. HAVE YOU EATEN?
    First timers favorite! Video with generation gaps, body-shaming, homophobia, slight verbal abuse.
2. ASIAN WOMEN ARE NOT___
    Othering, stereotyping, foreignness, and unassimilable nature.
3. ASIAN AMERICAN LOLITA EFFECT
    Fetishization, infantilization, and underage dating. Famous for easily consummable nature!
4. MODEL MINORITY MYTH SERIES
    A favorite among authentic Asian Americans! Video series with interviews, various definitions, mental health, and identity crises.
4. SELF CARE IS WARFARE
    New Museum special! Combo video and Instagram with self-care, solidarity, and collaboration.
4. PUT ASIAN FEMMES IN WHITE CUBES
    White cubes with performance art, protest, representation, and exclusion.

ILLEGIBLE BODIES SHOW
    New item. Racial triangulation, model minority myth with anti-blackness, and assimilation. Includes Illegible Bodies Magazine and Illegible Bodies Portraits.

ILLEGIBLE BODIES MAGAZINE
    New item. Interview questions on whiteness, anti-blackness, Chinese-ness, and queerness.

ILLEGIBLE BODIES PORTRAITS
    New item. Portraits of queer individuals navigating Western spaces with some awareness of their position in white Western spaces. 8 pieces
